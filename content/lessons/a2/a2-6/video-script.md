# Lesson A2-6: Formal Address and Cultural Etiquette

**Level:** A2
**Duration:** 40 minutes
**Module:** 2 | **Lesson:** 6
**Estimated Hours:** 0.67 hours

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:
- Understand the formal address system (Batono/Kalbatono)
- Know when to use formal vs. informal register
- Navigate social hierarchy in Georgian culture
- Use appropriate titles and address forms

## Key Concepts
- Formal address system
- Batono/Kalbatono
- Social hierarchy
- Cultural etiquette
- Register switching
