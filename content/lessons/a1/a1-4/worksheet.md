## Part 2: The Trilled R

Practice writing and saying the trilled რ [r]:

**Isolation practice:**
- რ - რ - რ - რ
- Hold it: rrrrrrrr

**Words with რ:**
1. __________ [ra] - 'what'
2. __________ [rogor] - 'how'
3. __________ [sakhl-i] - 'house'

**Troubleshooting:** If you can't trill, try:
- Saying 'drrrrr' (the 'd' helps position your tongue)
- Saying 'prrrrr' (the 'p' builds air pressure)
- Starting with a tap, then extending it

## Part 5: Consonant Clusters

**Practice strategy:**
1. Start slow: pronounce each consonant separately
2. Gradually speed up
3. Focus on smooth transitions

**Three-consonant clusters:**
- ღვთ [ɣvt] = __________ [ɣvtis] - 'God's'

**Four+ consonant clusters:**
- გრძ [grdz] = __________ [grdzeli] - 'long'

**Practice breaking down:**

## Answer Key

**Part 1:**
რ / [r] / Alveolar trill / Tongue vibrates
ღ / [ɣ] / Voiced guttural fricative / Throat sound with vibration
ხ / [x] / Voiceless guttural fricative / Throat sound without vibration

**Part 2:**
1. რა 2. როგორ 3. სახლი

**Part 3:**
ღ: Back of throat / Voiced / Clearing throat with voice
ხ: Back of throat / Voiceless / Clearing throat without voice
ღ = with vibration, ხ = without vibration

**Part 4:**
1. რ (რა) 2. ღ (ღამე) 3. ხ (ხარი) 4. ხ (ხელი) 5. რ (როგორ) 6. ხ (სახელი)

ღვთ: ღვთის
გრძ: გრძელი

გრძელი = grdzeli
მწვრთნელი = mts'vrtneli
