# Pronunciation Etiquette: Georgian Cultural Context

## Overview

Pronunciation in Georgian is deeply connected to social context. How you pronounce words, the formality level you use, and even regional variations all convey important cultural information. This guide helps learners understand when and how to adapt their pronunciation appropriately.
