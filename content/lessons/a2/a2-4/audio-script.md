## Section 1: Basic Verbs - To See (0:45 - 4:00)

### ხედვა [khedva] - To See

"ხედვა - ხედვა - ხედვა [xɛdvɑ]
ხედვა - to see

**[Repeat after me]**
ხედვა - ხედვა - ხედვა

**[Pronunciation tips]**
- Dictionary form (infinitive)
- Contains guttural ხ [x] sound
- Series I verb

### ვხედავ [vkhedav] - I See

"ვხედავ - ვხედავ - ვხედავ [vxɛdɑv]
ვხედავ - I see

**[Repeat after me]**
ვხედავ - ვხედავ - ვხედავ

**[Pronunciation breakdown]**
V-khe-dav

**[Pronunciation tips]**
- Notice the prefix ვ- [v] meaning 'I'
- First person singular present tense
- Contains guttural ხ [x] sound

**[Practice in context - Full sentence]**
მე ვხედავ კატას
me vkhedav kat'as
I see the cat

**[Repeat after me]**
მე ვხედავ კატას

**[Practice in context - Full sentence]**
მე ვხედავ სახლს
me vkhedav sakhl-s
I see the house

**[Repeat after me]**
მე ვხედავ სახლს"

## Section 2: Basic Verbs - To Read (4:00 - 7:30)

### კითხვა [kit'khva] - To Read

"კითხვა - კითხვა - კითხვა [kʼitʼxvɑ]
კითხვა - to read

**[Repeat after me]**
კითხვა - კითხვა - კითხვა

**[Pronunciation tips]**
- Dictionary form (infinitive)
- Contains ejective კ [kʼ] and ტ [tʼ] sounds
- Contains guttural ხ [x] sound
- Series I verb

### ვკითხულობ [vkit'khulob] - I Read

"ვკითხულობ - ვკითხულობ - ვკითხულობ [vkʼitʼxulɔb]
ვკითხულობ - I read

**[Repeat after me]**
ვკითხულობ - ვკითხულობ - ვკითხულობ

**[Pronunciation breakdown]**
v-kit'-KHU-lob

**[Pronunciation tips]**
- Notice the prefix ვ- [v] meaning 'I'
- First person singular present tense
- Contains ejective კ [kʼ] and ტ [tʼ] sounds
- Contains guttural ხ [x] sound
- Stress on third syllable: v-kit'-KHU-lob

**[Practice in context - Full sentence]**
მე ვკითხულობ წიგნს
me vkit'khulob ts'igns
I read the book

**[Repeat after me]**
მე ვკითხულობ წიგნს

**[Practice in context - Full sentence with time]**
მე ვკითხულობ წიგნს ყოველდღე
me vkit'khulob ts'igns q'oveldghe
I read the book every day

**[Repeat after me]**
მე ვკითხულობ წიგნს ყოველდღე"

## Section 3: Basic Verbs - To Give (7:30 - 10:30)

### მიცემა [mitsema] - To Give

"მიცემა - მიცემა - მიცემა [mitsɛmɑ]
მიცემა - to give

**[Repeat after me]**
მიცემა - მიცემა - მიცემა

**[Pronunciation tips]**
- Dictionary form (infinitive)
- No ejective sounds - easier to pronounce
- Series I verb with two objects (both Dative)

### ვაძლევ [vadzlev] - I Give

"ვაძლევ - ვაძლევ - ვაძლევ [vɑdzlɛv]
ვაძლევ - I give

**[Repeat after me]**
ვაძლევ - ვაძლევ - ვაძლევ

**[Pronunciation breakdown]**
VA-dzlev

**[Pronunciation tips]**
- Notice the prefix ვ- [v] meaning 'I'
- First person singular present tense
- Contains ძ [dz] sound (voiced 'dz')
- Stress on first syllable: VA-dzlev

**[Practice in context - Full sentence]**
მე ვაძლევ ბავშვს წიგნს
me vadzlev bavshvs ts'igns
I give the child a book

**[Repeat after me]**
მე ვაძლევ ბავშვს წიგნს

**[Practice in context - Full sentence]**
მე ვაძლევ საჩუქარს მეგობარს
me vadzlev sachuk'ars megobars
I give a gift to a friend

**[Repeat after me]**
მე ვაძლევ საჩუქარს მეგობარს"

## Section 4: Subject Pronouns (10:30 - 11:30)

### მე [me] - I

"მე - მე - მე [mɛ]
მე - I

**[Repeat after me]**
მე - მე - მე

**[Pronunciation tips]**
- First person singular pronoun
- Simple, clear pronunciation

**[Practice in context]**
მე ვარ აქ
me var ak
I am here

**[Repeat after me]**
მე ვარ აქ"

### შენ [shen] - You (Informal)

"შენ - შენ - შენ [ʃɛn]
შენ - you (singular, informal)

**[Repeat after me]**
შენ - შენ - შენ

**[Pronunciation tips]**
- Second person singular pronoun (informal)
- Contains შ [ʃ] sound (like 'sh' in 'shoe')
- Used with friends, family, peers

**[Practice in context]**
შენ ხარ კარგი
shen khar k'argi
You are good

**[Repeat after me]**
შენ ხარ კარგი"

### ის [is] - He, She, It

"ის - ის - ის [is]
ის - he, she, it

**[Repeat after me]**
ის - ის - ის

**[Pronunciation tips]**
- Third person singular pronoun
- No gender distinction in Georgian
- Simple, clear pronunciation

**[Practice in context]**
ის არის აქ
is aris ak
He/She is here

**[Repeat after me]**
ის არის აქ"

## Closing (12:30 - 13:00)

"Excellent practice! You've now practiced complete SVO sentences:

1. **Subject** (მე - I) - at the beginning
2. **Verb** (ვხედავ - I see) - with person prefix ვ-
3. **Object** (კატას - the cat) - in Dative case

Remember the pattern: **Subject + Verb + Object (Dative)**

Practice these sentence structures daily. In the next lesson, we'll learn high-frequency vocabulary!

Nakhvamdis!"

## Practice Tips

1. **Daily Review:** Practice SVO sentences 5-10 minutes daily
2. **Verb Conjugation:** Focus on the ვ- prefix meaning 'I'
3. **Case Practice:** Remember objects are in Dative case
4. **Full Sentences:** Always practice complete sentences, not just words
5. **Recording:** Record yourself saying complete sentences

---

## Notes for Audio Production

- **Pacing:** Natural pace, clear enunciation
- **Repetitions:** Say each form 3 times with brief pauses
- **Emphasis:** Clearly distinguish verb prefixes (ვ-, შ-, etc.)
- **Pauses:** Include 2-second pauses between sentences
- **Volume:** Normalized to -16 LUFS standard
- **Context:** Provide example sentences with natural intonation
- **Sentence Flow:** Practice complete sentences with natural rhythm
