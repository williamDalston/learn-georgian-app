# Lesson A2-5: High-Frequency Vocabulary (500 Words)

**Level:** A2
**Duration:** 60 minutes
**Module:** 2 | **Lesson:** 5
**Estimated Hours:** 1 hour

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:
- Acquire 50+ high-frequency Georgian words
- Understand vocabulary organization by theme (family, daily life, activities)
- Use new vocabulary in sentences with proper cases
- Build vocabulary systematically for 500-word goal

## Key Concepts
- Vocabulary acquisition strategies
- High-frequency words
- Daily life vocabulary
- Family terms
- Basic activities

### Main Content: Thematic Vocabulary Groups (0:30 - 60:00)

**Family Members:**
- დედა (deda) - mother
- მამა (mama) - father
- და (da) - sister
- ძმა (dzma) - brother
- ბებია (begia) - grandmother
- ბაბუა (babua) - grandfather

**Daily Activities:**
- ადიო (adio) - to go
- მოდის (modis) - comes
- აქვს (akvs) - has
- არის (aris) - is

**Common Objects:**
- მაგიდა (magida) - table
- სკამი (sk'ami) - chair
- კარი (k'ari) - door
