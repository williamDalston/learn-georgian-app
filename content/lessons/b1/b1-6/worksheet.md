## Tips for Success

1. **Practice conversations regularly** - Find a language partner or practice alone by playing both roles.

2. **Use follow-up questions** - Remember to ask 'áƒ¨áƒ”áƒœ?' (and you?) to keep conversations flowing naturally.

3. **Time expressions are key** - Use words like áƒ“áƒ¦áƒ”áƒ¡ (today), áƒ®áƒ•áƒáƒš (tomorrow), áƒ¨áƒ”áƒ›áƒ“áƒ”áƒ’ (then) to express time clearly.

4. **Combine verb types** - Use both Class I verbs (for actions) and Class IV verbs (for likes/knowledge) in conversations.

5. **Don't worry about perfection** - Focus on communication. It's okay to make mistakes as you learn!
