Listen to the difference:
ქალაქი [kʰalakʰi] - city (aspirated, with air)

## Section 4: Minimal Pairs Practice (8:00 - 10:00)

"Now let's practice minimal pairs - words that differ only in one sound:

## Closing (11:30 - 12:00)

"Great practice! Remember:
- Voiced sounds: feel vibration in your throat
- Aspirated sounds: feel air puff (paper moves)
- Ejective sounds: feel throat pop (sharp release)

Keep practicing these distinctions daily. They're crucial for proper Georgian pronunciation. See you in the next lesson!"

---

## Practice Tips

1. **Daily practice:** 5-10 minutes per day with this audio guide
2. **Paper test:** Hold paper in front of mouth to check aspiration
3. **Throat check:** Place hand on throat to feel voiced vs voiceless
4. **Minimal pairs:** Practice contrasting words side-by-side
5. **Recording:** Record yourself and compare to native pronunciation
