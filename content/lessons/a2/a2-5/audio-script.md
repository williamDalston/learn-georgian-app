## Section 1: Family Members (0:45 - 4:00)

### დედა [deda] - Mother

"დედა - დედა - დედა [dɛdɑ]
დედა - mother

**[Repeat after me]**
დედა - დედა - დედა

**[Pronunciation tips]**
- No ejective sounds - easier to pronounce
- Short, clear word
- Essential family term

**[Practice in context]**
დედა სახლშია
deda sakhlshia
Mother is at home

**[Repeat after me]**
დედა სახლშია"

### მამა [mama] - Father

## Practice Tips

1. **Daily Review:** Practice vocabulary 10-15 minutes daily
2. **Thematic Groups:** Learn related words together
3. **Context Practice:** Always use words in complete sentences
4. **Frequency Focus:** Prioritize the most common words
5. **Recording:** Record yourself saying vocabulary words and sentences

---

## Notes for Audio Production

- **Pacing:** Natural pace, clear enunciation
- **Repetitions:** Say each word 3 times with brief pauses
- **Emphasis:** Clearly pronounce each word
- **Pauses:** Include 2-second pauses between word groups
- **Volume:** Normalized to -16 LUFS standard
- **Context:** Provide example sentences with natural intonation
- **Thematic Organization:** Group related vocabulary together
