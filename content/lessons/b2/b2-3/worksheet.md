---

**Key Rules to Remember:**
- Ergative = Transitive verbs in Series II/III
- Nominative = Series I OR intransitive verbs (all series)
- Most nouns: drop -ი, add -მა
- Third person pronouns: ის → მან, ისინი → მათ
